Too Many Losing Heroines! (Japanese: 負けヒロインが多すぎる！) The Japanese light novel series, written by Takibi Amamori and illustrated by Imigimuru. It tells the story of a high school boy who meets several girls dealing with heartbreak after being rejected by their crushes. The novel started under Shogakukan's Gagaga Bunko label in July 2021, and a manga version by Itachi began on Shogakukan’s Ura Sunday website and MangaOne app in April 2022. An anime adaptation by A-1 Pictures aired from July to September 2024.
